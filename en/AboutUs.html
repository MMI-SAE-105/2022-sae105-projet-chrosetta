Who am I ?

I am a student in first year of BUT MMI. I am part of the C1 group.

The treatment of the subject

The subject of the project is: "notes for later". So we have to design a website that the us of the future could read again. I want that for this me of the future, to recall a concept that is important to me right now. The principle of positivity and always moving forward. In order to respect the constraints of the subject, I show these principles through various subjects. I set the scene with sacred places of the Buddhist religion. This same religion which advocates ideals close to my vision. The book also joins its ideas and develops the fact of always moving forward to discover more. The music and the chosen film illustrate these notions in a pragmatic way. Which shows that it is the experience and the lived that brings knowledge. Finally, to come back to positivism. In order to move forward, one must know how to put things into perspective and this is what the first 4 articles show. However, one should not push the notion of positivity to the extreme as some personal development programs may encourage. One must know how to appreciate each moment, but not fall into toxic positivity either. Finally, the subject of new technologies might seem far from these notions. However, they show that the world continues to evolve. Certainly, an evolution less focused on spirituality. But rather on the development of technologies, which bring to the disabled people, the means to bloom with their differences without being disadvantaged compared to the valid people.
