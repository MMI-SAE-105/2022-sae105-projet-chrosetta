Forrest Gump
an instructive work

Forrest Gump perfectly illustrates the phrase "if you want it, you can do it". Thanks to his will and his determination, he continues to move forward.

Its summary

The movie Forrest Gump tells the story of one man's life. Forrest is a simple man with a below average IQ (75). At first glance, nothing destined him for great things. However, despite his handicap, he ends up doing great things. He even participates in major events in his country (the United States). The film was released in 1994. In 1995, it was awarded six film Oscars, including the one for best film. To watch the trailer

A pragmatic vision

The movie Forrest Gump is full of inspiring quotes. Let's take a closer look and explain them : Let's start with the most famous one of all.

"LIFE IS LIKE A BOX OF CHOCOLATES. YOU NEVER KNOW WHAT YOU'RE GOING TO GET".

This sentence shows the very pragmatic side of Forrest Gump. Indeed, he does not think about things, he takes things as they come and advises. This quote shows that life also has different flavors. And in order to know its flavors, we have to test them, otherwise, we will never know them.

"shit happens"

This quote, translated in the French version as "ça arrive". It is again a very pragmatic vision of events. Bad things can happen, but that's the way it is. It is normal. You have to accept it. Life is not made of only good moments.

"YOU HAVE TO LEAVE THE PAST BEHIND IF YOU WANT TO MOVE FORWARD."

This quote shows us that past events belong to the past. That we cannot change them. We must know how to live with them and learn to move forward despite everything. We must not worry about the past. This message could seem sad. However, it shows an optimistic vision. Since the past is an unchangeable thing. It is better to focus on the future. What awaits us is in front of us, and depends only on us.
